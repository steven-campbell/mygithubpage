Steven Campbell: 2018 SAA Application Creative Piece

For many years, I have been obsessed with fractals, the patterns they show and reveal, their connections to nature, and how math connects to nature in these beatiful designs.

If you don't know much about fractals, they all share some common qualities, like self-similarity and a pictorial representation.

Looking at this tree and adjusting some parameters, I noticed some things with self-similarity and changing some of the parameters. Trees are often good metaphors for life, and this fractal tree is no exception.
